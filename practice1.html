Simplifying your tax filing, one return at a time

B. Kabir & Law Associates provides tailored tax advisory and compliance services designed to meet the evolving needs of individuals, businesses, and multinational firms. Our tax practice focuses on accuracy, foresight, and alignment with local and international tax regulations. We support clients through proactive tax planning, personalized advisory, and full-spectrum representation. From expatriate filings to high-level tribunal matters, we ensure our clients remain fully compliant while optimizing their tax outcomes. We act as both advisors and advocates, helping clients minimize risks, leverage opportunities, and navigate an increasingly complex tax landscape.

Our Tax Services Include

Preparation and filing of income tax returns for resident and non-resident individuals, ensuring compliance with Bangladesh’s tax framework.

Evaluation and optimization of corporate tax liabilities through efficient planning and regulatory alignment.

Advisory and compliance services for capital gains derived from asset or investment disposals.

Support in structuring and reporting inherited assets, ensuring legal and fiscal compliance.

Tax management for trusts and estate transfers, including fiduciary responsibilities and beneficiary obligations.

Calculation and application of deferred tax balances in line with accounting and tax standards.

Acting on behalf of clients before tax authorities, including audits, filings, and formal communications.

Advising partnerships on tax allocations, obligations, and compliance strategies.

Filing and managing appeals against tax authority decisions.

Representation before appellate tax forums for dispute resolution.

Handling escalated tax disputes referred to the High Court.
